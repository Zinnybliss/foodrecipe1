MY FAVOURITE MEAL

The food I love the most is Fufu with Oha soup.


ORIGIN OF THE FOOD

Oha soup is popular amongst the Igbo tribes in Nigeria, and this group dominated the eastern part of the country
Oha leaves are gotten from the Oha tree, and also fufu is gotten from cassava.


Ingredients Measurement
Oha Leaves 1 bowl
Stock Fish 6 pieces
Dry Fish 6 pieces
Beef 10 pieces
Goat meat 12 pieces
Crayfish 3 tablespoon
Fresh pepper 1 Handful
Ogiri 2 wraps
Maggi 2 cubes
Palm oil 1 cup
Cocoyam 4 pieces

HOW TO PREPARE THE MEAL

  • Wash your cocoyam and put inside pot and allow to boil for 15-20minutes
  • Bring down the cocoyam and pound it to a paste.
  • Wash your meat and fish and set to boil for 20-25 minutes to allow it soften well.
  • Add your salt, spices, ogiri and pepper
  • Add the cocoyam paste and oil
  • Allow to boil for 15 minutes
  • Add your oha leaves and let it simmer for 2 minutes
  • Turn everything together and bring it down.
